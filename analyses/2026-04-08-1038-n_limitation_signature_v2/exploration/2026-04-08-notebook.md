## 2026-04-08 10:45 — Step 1: KG discovery

### Method
Queried `list_experiments(organism="MED4", verbose=True, limit=None)` and `list_publications(organism="MED4")`. Then used `differential_expression_by_gene(summary=True)` per experiment to get gene counts.

### Outputs
- [experiment_scoping.csv](../data/experiment_scoping.csv) — 10 experiments classified

### QC
- Total MED4 experiments in KG: 33 across 13 publications
- Treatment types: nitrogen (8), light (8), carbon (4), coculture (3), viral (3), iron (3), salt (1), phosphorus (1), diel (1), growth_phase (1)
- Table scopes: all_detected_genes (10), filtered_subset (12), significant_only (6), significant_any_timepoint (5)

### Exploration

**Gene count survey** revealed major coverage differences:
- Tolonen N-dep and cyanate/urea: 1,697 genes (full genome, all_detected_genes)
- Read N-depleted: 857 genes (filtered_subset, top 50% by expression)
- Weissberg RNA-seq: 1,849 genes; proteomics: 1,424 genes
- Phosphate starvation: only 34 genes — unusable for scoring
- Iron starvation: 112 genes, significant_any_timepoint — unusable
- Light experiments: 198 genes (filtered_subset)
- Viral: 25-45 genes (significant_only) — too sparse

**Key insight:** Most non-nitrogen experiments have limited gene coverage (filtered_subset or significant_only), making them poor controls for rank-based scoring. The KG lacks well-powered non-N stress experiments for MED4 — noted for gaps_and_friction.md.

**Control selection rationale:**
- Tolonen cyanate + urea: same lab, same platform, N-sufficient, full gene coverage. Best negatives.
- Aharonovich coculture: MED4+HOT1A3 without N-limitation. Tests whether coculture alone triggers signature.
- High white light (Steglich): only 198 genes but tests N-specificity of the signature. If it scores high, our signature captures general stress.

**Marker gene confirmation:**
- PMM0920 (glnA): glutamine synthetase, type I ✓
- PMM0370 (cynA): cyanate ABC transporter, substrate-binding protein ✓
- PMM0550 (rbcL): ribulose bisphosphate carboxylase, large subunit ✓
- PMM1452 (atpD): ATP synthase delta chain ✓
- PMM0030: conserved hypothetical protein (unnamed) ✓
- PMM0346: conserved hypothetical protein (unnamed, rank 1 down, in proteomics) ✓ — added to cover proteomics edge case

**Marker gene presence across selected experiments:**

- High white light (198-gene filtered subset): no marker genes present. Can still score but can't trace markers.
- Weissberg proteomics: PMM0030 (uncharacterized) not detected — expected for low-abundance protein.

**Diel cycle concern — resolved:**
Read 2017 has diel periodicity data in the KG (Zinser 2009 diel clusters, 18 clusters, 1,756 genes). All 198 v1 core signature genes have diel cluster assignments. However, both Read 2017 and Tolonen 2006 used **continuous light** (~55 and 50 µmol m⁻² s⁻¹ respectively), so diel cycling was not active during either experiment. Weissberg 2025 also used continuous light (20 µmol). The diel cluster assignments reflect real biology but do not confound this analysis.

### Decision
**References:** Tolonen N-dep, Read N-depleted (only two N-deprivation studies for MED4 in the KG).
**Negative controls:** Tolonen cyanate, Tolonen urea, Aharonovich coculture, High white light.
**Targets:** Weissberg × 4 (RNA-seq + proteomics, axenic + coculture).
**Positive controls:** Reference studies scored per-timepoint (self-scoring with known circularity).
**Skipped:** Phosphate (34 genes), iron (112 genes), salt (28 genes), viral (25-45 genes), carbon (no non-sig rows), remaining light experiments. Gap logged.

### Decision
Proceed to signature building. Data is clean and marker genes behave as expected.

## 2026-04-08 11:30 — Step 3: Build signature

### Command
```bash
uv run analyses/.../scripts/03_build_signature.py --explore
```

### Inputs
- `data/de_ref_tolonen_ndep.csv` — filtered to 6h-48h (excluded 0h, 3h)
- `data/de_ref_read_ndep.csv` — filtered to 12h-24h (excluded 3h)

### Outputs
- `data/core_signature.csv` — 189 genes (74 up, 115 down)
- `data/extended_signature.csv` — 342 genes
- `data/discordant_genes.csv` — 4 genes

### QC — Filter funnel
- Tolonen (6h-48h): 409 significant genes (196 up, 213 down)
- Read (12h-24h): 319 significant genes (134 up, 185 down)
- Core (concordant intersection): **189 genes** (74 up, 115 down)
- Extended: 342 genes (143 tolonen_only_read_ns, 114 read_only_tolonen_ns, 73 tolonen_only_read_absent, 12 read_only_tolonen_absent)
- Discordant: 4 genes

### Exploration

**Read 3h exclusion rationale:** Read 3h had 101 DE genes with 2:1 up/down bias. Photosynthesis genes (psbX, rbcL) showed transient upregulation at 3h that reversed by 12h. This caused rbcL to be classified as discordant (up in Read due to tie-break, down in Tolonen). Excluding Read 3h parallels Tolonen 0h/3h exclusion — both are transient early responses, not sustained N-deprivation.

**Symmetric extended classification:** Added `study_a_all_locus_tags` parameter so B-only genes get the same `_ns`/`_absent` classification as A-only genes. Result: 12 Read-only genes are absent from Tolonen's microarray entirely.

**Rank distribution:** median=25, 41% of genes have rank ≤20. 7 rank-1 genes including cynA, rbcL, atpD, atpF, PMM0030, PMM0337, PMM0346.

**All 6 marker genes in CORE:**

| Marker | Direction | Tolonen rank | Read rank | Cross rank |
|--------|-----------|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| glnA (PMM0920) | up | 2 | 42 | 2 |
| cynA (PMM0370) | up | 1 | 14 | 1 |
| rbcL (PMM0550) | down | 1 | 121 | 1 |
| atpD (PMM1452) | down | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| PMM0030 | up | 1 | 2 | 1 |
| PMM0346 | down | 113 | 1 | 1 |

### Decision
Signature is biologically sound. 189 core genes with all expected markers. Proceed to target extraction and scoring.
