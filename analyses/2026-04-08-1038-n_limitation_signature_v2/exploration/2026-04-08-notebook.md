### Exploration

**Gene count survey** revealed major coverage differences:
- Tolonen N-dep and cyanate/urea: 1,697 genes (full genome, all_detected_genes)
- Read N-depleted: 857 genes (filtered_subset, top 50% by expression)
- Weissberg RNA-seq: 1,849 genes; proteomics: 1,424 genes
- Phosphate starvation: only 34 genes — unusable for scoring
- Iron starvation: 112 genes, significant_any_timepoint — unusable
- Light experiments: 198 genes (filtered_subset)
- Viral: 25-45 genes (significant_only) — too sparse

**Key insight:** Most non-nitrogen experiments have limited gene coverage (filtered_subset or significant_only), making them poor controls for rank-based scoring. The KG lacks well-powered non-N stress experiments for MED4 — noted for gaps_and_friction.md.

**Control selection rationale:**
- Tolonen cyanate + urea: same lab, same platform, N-sufficient, full gene coverage. Best negatives.
- Aharonovich coculture: MED4+HOT1A3 without N-limitation. Tests whether coculture alone triggers signature.
- High white light (Steglich): only 198 genes but tests N-specificity of the signature. If it scores high, our signature captures general stress.

**Marker gene confirmation:**
- PMM0920 (glnA): glutamine synthetase, type I ✓

### Decision
**References:** Tolonen N-dep, Read N-depleted (only two N-deprivation studies for MED4 in the KG).
**Negative controls:** Tolonen cyanate, Tolonen urea, Aharonovich coculture, High white light.
**Targets:** Weissberg × 4 (RNA-seq + proteomics, axenic + coculture).
**Positive controls:** Reference studies scored per-timepoint (self-scoring with known circularity).
**Skipped:** Phosphate (34 genes), iron (112 genes), salt (28 genes), viral (25-45 genes), carbon (no non-sig rows), remaining light experiments. Gap logged.
