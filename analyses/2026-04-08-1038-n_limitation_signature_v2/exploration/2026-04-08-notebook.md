**Key insight:** Most non-nitrogen experiments have limited gene coverage (filtered_subset or significant_only), making them poor controls for rank-based scoring. The KG lacks well-powered non-N stress experiments for MED4 тАФ noted for gaps_and_friction.md.

**Control selection rationale:**
- Tolonen cyanate + urea: same lab, same platform, N-sufficient, full gene coverage. Best negatives.
- Aharonovich coculture: MED4+HOT1A3 without N-limitation. Tests whether coculture alone triggers signature.
- High white light (Steglich): only 198 genes but tests N-specificity of the signature. If it scores high, our signature captures general stress.

**Diel cycle concern тАФ resolved:**
Read 2017 has diel periodicity data in the KG (Zinser 2009 diel clusters, 18 clusters, 1,756 genes). All 198 v1 core signature genes have diel cluster assignments. However, both Read 2017 and Tolonen 2006 used **continuous light** (~55 and 50 ┬╡mol mтБ╗┬▓ sтБ╗┬╣ respectively), so diel cycling was not active during either experiment. Weissberg 2025 also used continuous light (20 ┬╡mol). The diel cluster assignments reflect real biology but do not confound this analysis.

### Decision
**References:** Tolonen N-dep, Read N-depleted (only two N-deprivation studies for MED4 in the KG).
**Negative controls:** Tolonen cyanate, Tolonen urea, Aharonovich coculture, High white light.
**Targets:** Weissberg ├Ч 4 (RNA-seq + proteomics, axenic + coculture).
**Positive controls:** Reference studies scored per-timepoint (self-scoring with known circularity).
**Skipped:** Phosphate (34 genes), iron (112 genes), salt (28 genes), viral (25-45 genes), carbon (no non-sig rows), remaining light experiments. Gap logged.
