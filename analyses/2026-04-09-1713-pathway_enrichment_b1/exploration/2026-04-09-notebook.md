### Outputs

- `results/heatmap_enrichment_down.png/.svg` — down-enriched pathways, all experiments
- `results/heatmap_enrichment_signed.png/.svg` — signed enrichment score, all experiments × timepoints
- `results/discordance_scatter.png/.svg` — pathway-level RNA vs protein enrichment (coculture)
- `results/trajectory_proteomics_coculture.png/.svg` — E.4/Q.1/K.2 enrichment trajectory across timepoints
- `results/control_separation.png/.svg` — negative control vs target experiment separation

### Visualization iterations [interpretation]

**Iteration 1 — separate up/down heatmaps.** Readable but require side-by-side comparison to see discordance. Retained as supplementary figures.

**Iteration 2 — signed enrichment score.** Single heatmap with sign derived from the dominant direction (up vs down wins when both are significant). Captures the full story: discordance, controls, direction, magnitude. This became the primary figure.

**Signed score construction:** For each pathway × experiment × timepoint, take the -log10(padj) from the winning direction test and assign +/- sign. If both up and down are significant, the direction with smaller padj wins. If neither is significant, score = 0. This loses some information (when both directions significant) but is rare in practice and the gain in interpretability is large.

**Iteration 3 — discordance scatter.** X-axis: RNA-seq coculture enrichment score (averaged over timepoints). Y-axis: proteomics coculture score. Labeled points for E.4, Q.1, K.2. Shows clearly that E.4 and Q.1 live in the upper-left quadrant (high protein, low RNA).

**Iteration 4 — trajectory plot.** E.4 (N-metabolism), Q.1 (transport), K.2 (ribosomal) trajectories in proteomics coculture across d5/d18/d31/d60/d89. Shows persistence of N-stress signal across all timepoints. K.2 ribosomal shutdown begins at d31 and deepens.

**Iteration 5 — control separation.** Negative controls (Aharonovich, Steglich, Tolonen urea) plotted vs targets and reference. Confirms clean separation except cyanate (partial N-stress as expected). [KG]

### Decision
Signed heatmap is the primary figure. Discordance scatter and trajectory plots support the main finding. Control separation confirms analysis integrity. Finalize documentation.
